About Elizabeth Xi Bauer

 

Founded in 2015, Elizabeth Xi Bauer began as an innovative online platform accompanied by pop-up exhibitions. In 2021, as the UK was exiting lockdown restrictions, the gallery took on the challenge to open a permanent space in South-East London. Since then, as well as an exhibition programme, Elizabeth Xi Bauer has collaborated on projects with international institutions, curators, and artists across various cities, such as São Paulo, Amsterdam, Brussels, and Lisbon.

 

In 2025, to mark its tenth anniversary, Elizabeth Xi Bauer opened a second gallery space in London’s vibrant Exmouth Market, in Clerkenwell. Expanding from its original home in Deptford, this new location furthered the gallery’s mission to foster cutting-edge contemporary art and support both emerging and established artists on a global stage.   

 

Elizabeth Xi Bauer has been instrumental in launching the careers of emerging artists, such as Theodore Ereira-Guyer and Abraham Kritzman, and working with a diverse range of global artists, such as Antonio Pichillá and Karoliina Hellberg. The Gallery's represented artists have exhibited around the world, and their works have been acquired by institutions including the Tate, Victoria and Albert Museum, and British Museum. 

How We Help Our Clients

 

Elizabeth Xi Bauer provides collectors and investors with direct access to museum-quality art. This access to validated artists has contributed to our clients seeing significant increases in the value of their artworks.

 

Through the Artistic programme, Elizabeth Xi Bauer builds awareness and demand from the Art world experts, who run the most respected institutions. Our research clearly indicates this is the most vital factor for artists and their markets to stand the test of time.

Next steps

 

We understand that everyone's buying journey is different and we pride ourselves on providing a personalised experience, ensuring clients are comfortable at every stage. A typical path looks like this.

 

Consultation

We provide a bespoke buying experience to collectors and investors. A member of the Private Collections team will reach out to discuss your needs, requirements and goals and then provide a proposal that meets your needs.

 

Purchase

Once you are happy with the artwork(s) you are purchasing, you will reserve the artwork with a 10% deposit. You will then complete our anti-money laundering process (as an Art Market Participant, regulated by HMRC, we adhere to straight AML policies). Once complete, you are free to settle the balance of your artwork(s)

 

Your Artwork

Once you have paid for your artwork and received your Certificate of Authenticity and Letter of Provenance, you will be the sole owner of the artwork(s). You can collect your artwork or we can advise on delivery options, alternatively, many clients utilise our three years free storage and insurance with third-party providers.

 

Aftercare

You will be kept up to date by our private collections team on the progress of the gallery and our artists, you can decide the frequency and we recommend attending our exhibition openings when you can.

 

Exit

Our terms and conditions state that clients must hold their artwork for a minimum of three years to preserve the integrity of an artist's market. After those three years, you are free to reach out to your Private Collections Manager to discuss your options or you can choose to keep your artwork for longer to benefit from greater returns.
