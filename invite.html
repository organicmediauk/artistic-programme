This latest exhibition from Pichillá Quiacaín also coincides with the release of Pichillá's publication, Ombligo Tierra (Navel-Earth), marking a significant milestone in the artist's career. The book offers an insight into Pichillá's career and a behind-the-scenes look at his artistic process.

In front of the lake - Antonio Pichillá Quiacaín, 2023        

Elizabeth Xi Bauer is also thrilled to announce the representation of Antonio Pichillá Quiacaín, who is at a key point in his artistic career. Recently featured by Forbes and the London Art Fair, Pichillá is solidifying himself as an internationally renowned textile artist.
