Dear Mr/Ms,

 

Thank you for your time today, I wanted to share the simple steps, that our valuable client’s experience with Elizabeth Xi Bauer, to give you peace of mind and a better understanding of its simplicity.

 

Step 1 – A relaxed, informative consultation, in which I can understand your needs and discuss with you how Elizabeth Xi Bauer could potentially meet those needs.


Step 2 - After I have established your requirements, I will be able to confirm if the art opportunities can work for you and look for a specific piece, by an artist that matches your needs. Once a piece becomes available, I will reach out, take a small deposit to reserve your artwork. Once reserved, I’ll guide you through Anti-Money Laundering requirements and upon completion of this step, you’ll be free to settle the balance.


Step 3 - Once you have settled the balance, your artwork is held under your sole ownership. Many of my clients take advantage of the three years of free storage and insurance, offered by Elizabeth Xi Bauer, but if you would like to take delivery of your art, that can also be arranged.


Step 4 - A large part of my role, starts once you have purchased your art, where I will check in at a frequency of your choosing, invite you to events, give you updates on the gallery and its artists and, if you chose, to facilitate the sale of your artwork.

 

I look forward to embarking on this journey together and as you enter the art investment world with Elizabeth Xi Bauer, please rest assured, that you are in good hands. Naturally, if you have any questions regarding the process or any related subject, please don’t hesitate to call me at your convenience.
